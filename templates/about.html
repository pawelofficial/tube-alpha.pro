About Tube Alpha

What this tool does and what it doesn't

What is Tube Alpha?

Tube Alpha scrapes financial YouTube channels, downloads transcripts, and uses AI to identify every asset mentioned — stocks, crypto, commodities — along with whether the host was bullish or bearish on it. The result is a quick sentiment summary so you can scan what analysts are talking about without watching hours of video.

How it works

  • We monitor a curated list of financial YouTube channels.
  • New videos are automatically picked up and their transcripts extracted.
  • GPT-4o reads each transcript and identifies asset mentions with sentiment.
  • Results appear on the Dashboard, updated continuously as new videos land.
  • You can also paste any YouTube URL yourself to analyse a video on demand.

⚠️ Not financial advice

Everything on this platform is for informational and entertainment purposes only. Tube Alpha does not provide investment advice, financial guidance, or trading recommendations. Sentiment extracted from YouTube videos reflects the opinions of the original content creators — not ours, and not yours.

Always do your own research. Never make investment decisions based solely on what a YouTuber says, and never based on an AI summary of what a YouTuber says. Past sentiment is not indicative of future performance.

Subscription

A subscription gives you full access to the Dashboard (all channels, all assets, historical sentiment) and unlimited on-demand video analysis. Free users can try a limited number of video analyses to see how it works before committing.
